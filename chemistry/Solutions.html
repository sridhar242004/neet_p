Solutions

In chemistry, solutions refer to homogeneous mixtures composed of two or more substances, where one substance (the solute) is uniformly dispersed in another substance (the solvent). These mixtures can vary in concentration, allowing for precise control in industries like pharmaceuticals, food production, and environmental sciences. Understanding solutions is crucial in determining properties such as solubility, viscosity, and conductivity, influencing everything from drug formulations to pollution remediation techniques.
