Surface Chemistry

Surface chemistry deals with the study of chemical processes that occur at the interfaces of substances. It explores phenomena such as adsorption, catalysis, and reactions on surfaces, crucial for understanding various industrial processes, environmental remediation, and biological systems. Surface chemistry plays a pivotal role in designing materials with specific properties and enhancing the efficiency of chemical reactions in diverse applications.
