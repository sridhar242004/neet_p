Hydrocarbons

Hydrocarbons are organic compounds consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon atoms bonded together in various configurations. They form the basis of fossil fuels like petroleum and natural gas, as well as essential molecules in organic chemistry, ranging from simple alkanes to complex aromatic structures. Hydrocarbons play a crucial role in industry, energy production, and everyday life, serving as fuels, solvents, and raw materials for a wide array of chemical processes.
