Biomolecules

In chemistry, biomolecules are organic molecules essential for life, encompassing a diverse range of substances like carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, and nucleic acids. They play crucial roles in cellular structure, energy storage, and genetic information transfer, forming the foundation of biological processes and functions in living organisms.
