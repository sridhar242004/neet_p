Solid State

Solid state chemistry explores the properties, structures, and behaviors of solid materials, focusing on their atomic and molecular arrangements within a crystalline or amorphous state. It encompasses the study of solids' physical, chemical, and electronic properties, crucial for understanding materials ranging from semiconductors to ceramics and beyond.
