Aldehydes, Ketones, and Carboxylic Acids

Aldehydes, ketones, and carboxylic acids are important classes of organic compounds, each distinguished by their unique functional groups and chemical properties. Aldehydes feature a carbonyl group bonded to at least one hydrogen atom, while ketones have a carbonyl group bonded to two carbon atoms. Carboxylic acids contain a carboxyl group, consisting of a carbonyl group bonded to a hydroxyl group. These compounds play crucial roles in biological processes, industrial applications, and everyday chemistry, showcasing diverse reactivity and structural versatility in organic chemistry.
