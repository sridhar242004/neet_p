Chemistry in Everyday Life

Chemistry in Everyday Life explores how chemical principles impact our daily routines, from the food we eat and the medicines we take to the products we use and the environment we live in. It highlights the essential role of chemistry in improving health, enhancing technology, and shaping sustainable practices for a better quality of life.
