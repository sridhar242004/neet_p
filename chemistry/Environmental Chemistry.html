Environmental Chemistry

Environmental chemistry is the study of chemical processes occurring in the environment, focusing on understanding how pollutants arise, transform, and impact ecosystems and human health. It examines the interactions between chemical elements, compounds, and natural systems, aiming to mitigate environmental degradation and promote sustainable practices.
