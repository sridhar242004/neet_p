Understanding the Physical World

The physical world encompasses all matter and energy in the universe. It's governed by fundamental laws of physics that describe phenomena ranging from subatomic particles to cosmic structures. Key concepts include:

  • Matter and its properties
  • Energy and its various forms
  • Forces and interactions
  • Space and time

Understanding these concepts is crucial for NEET aspirants as they form the foundation of advanced physics topics.
